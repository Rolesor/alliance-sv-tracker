Alliance SV Daily Growth (Shared)

Track your daily Share Value (SV) and visualize its growth.

How to use:

  1. Enter the daily SV in the field below.
  2. Click 'Add Day' to save the data.
  3. The chart and table will update automatically.
  4. Click 'Delete' next to any entry to remove it.
